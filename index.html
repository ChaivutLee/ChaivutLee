About Me

My name is Chaivut Lee and I am currently studying Computer Programming at Saint Paul College in St. Paul, Minnesota. My planned graduation date is May 2025 with the Computer Programing AAS degree. I am mainly focusing on programming and web design. I made this website as a portfolio of my skills developing websites.
I have taken the following classes at Saint Paul College:

  • Web Fundamentals, where I learned HTML and CSS
  • Computer Science and Information Systems
  • Computer Networking
Next semester I will be taking Client Side Programming, Database Management Fundamentals, and Computing and Programming Concepts. I am also planning to take Java before I graduate.

My hobbies include video gaming and fishing.
